Dimensions V

Choreographic research and study on the original musical composition by Theodoros Gkugousoudis based on Igor Stravinki's methodology in the frame of the programme Stravinsky Miniatures of the Greek National Opera, with the participation of students of the Greek National Opera and musicians of the Athens Symphony Youth Orchestra. The artistic result of Stravinsky Miniatures was captured in an original video and screened in the context of World Dance Day by the Stavros Niarchos Foundation Cultural Centre and the Greek National Opera. The work was screened again at Flux Laboratory Athens as part of the Field Trip programme.
